KASI Lending Environment Simulator Tool helps banks and financials predict a credit score of the target group of people in a given area manipulating multiple lending criteria The credit officer at a branch will just have to answer 9 questions every 3 months and the model will return a rating as follows

  • Very poor - Less than 350
  • Poor - Between 350 and 550
  • Fair - Between 550 and 650
  • Good - Between 650 and 750
  • Very Good - Higher than 750
